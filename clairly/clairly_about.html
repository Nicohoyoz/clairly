about clairly.

our mission

Clairly is an AI-powered assistant built specifically for Montclair State University students. We believe that technology should enhance the student experience, making campus life easier, more accessible, and more connected.

what we offer

  • 24/7 assistance with university-related questions
  • Information about campus resources and services
  • Guidance on academic policies and procedures
  • Help navigating student life at Montclair
  • Quick answers to common student questions

built by students, for students

Clairly was created by Montclair students who understand the challenges of university life. We're constantly improving and adding features based on student feedback to make your college experience better.
